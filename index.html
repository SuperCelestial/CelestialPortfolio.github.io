Celestial - A Data Analyst

Uncovering Patterns, Empowering Strategies

about me

Hello! I'm Celestial, a recent graduate in BSc Computer Science, eagerly venturing into the world of data analysis. With a solid foundation gained through a Google data analytics course, I took on a three-month internship where I gained hands-on experience. I worked on diverse projects, including data scraping, interactive dashboard creation with Plotly, news website scraping, bike share analysis, and a college project on sentiment analysis.

Throughout my final year, I managed my studies alongside these projects and expanded my skill set by mastering SQL, earning three HackerRank certificates. Currently, I am focused on enhancing my analytical abilities through statistical studies and actively engaging in excel-based projects to explore new dimensions in data analysis.

Moreover, I have even started blogging about the things I am learning, primarily related to data analysis. It allows me to share my insights and knowledge with others, contributing to the data analyst community.

I am passionate about extracting hidden insights from data and transforming them into meaningful actions. Every project is an opportunity to push boundaries, learn, and grow in the dynamic field of data analysis. Thank you for getting to know me, and I'm excited to contribute my skills and knowledge to future data-driven endeavors.
